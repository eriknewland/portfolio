Overview

SpreadShot was born out of my personal need to deal with the modern hiring paradigm. Job seekers often send out over 100 applications before getting hired, and this can be an incredibly time consuming process, especially if they're applying with a unique cover letter and/or resume. SpreadShot scrapes the web for relevant jobs and allows users to generate cover letters, resumes, and potential interview questions for each job. Under the hood, SpreadShot features a complex architecture and deployment that utilizes multiple micro-services to create a tiered SAAS product website. SpreadShot also scores 99 out of 100 when testing with Lighthouse.

SpreadShot is perfect for all job seekers, whether you're seeking your first job in industry or looking for a senior or specialized position. My goal is to make it easier for everyone to get hired, and get hired faster.
